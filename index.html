EXPLORE WAYANAD!

Wayanad is a district in the state of Kerala, India, known for its natural beauty, wildlife, and caves.This area is renowned with having world's largest recorded population of Asian Elephants and Tigers.

Top Places to go

BANASURA SAGAR DAM

It is the largest earth dam in India and the second largest of its kind in Asia.

KARLAD LAKE

Karlad Lake in Wayanad district is the second largest freshwater lake in Kerala.

EDAKKAL CAVES

Edakkal Caves are among the oldest human settlements ever discovered.

KURUVA ISLANDS

The island is covered with thick and green forests all around.

CHEMBRA PEAK

Chembra Peak is the highest mountain peak in Wayanad. It is 2100 m above sea level.

900 KANDI

The glass bridge at 900 Kandi is one of the most popular attractions, offering a unique and thrilling experience.

POOKODE LAKE

Pookode Lake in Wayanad is a freshwater lake near Kalpetta resembling the map of India.

MUTHANGA WILDLIFE

Muthanga Wildlife Sanctuary is a rainforest which connects Nagarhole and Bandipur and Mudumalai Forests.

BRAHMAGIRI HILLS

Brahmagiri, is a mountain range in the Western Ghats of south India.

THIRUNELLI TEMPLE

Thirunelli Temple (also spelled Tirunelli) is an ancient temple dedicated to Maha Vishnu on the side of Brahmagiri hill in Kerala

PHANTOM ROCK

The towering Phantom Rock and its surroundings are popular among nature lovers and photographers.

KURUMBALAKOTTA HILLS

One of the best place in wayanad to see the sunrise view.
